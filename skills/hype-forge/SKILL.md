## Operational Standards
- **Value Bridge Requirement**: Every technical feature mentioned must explain its User Benefit and Business/Strategic Impact.
- **Platform Personas**: Strictly adhere to the unique voice and constraints of each platform (e.g., no fluff on HN, punchy on X).
